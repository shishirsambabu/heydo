## The thesis

> **India has 500 million skilled workers. Less than 2% have a verified digital identity that lets them earn trust-based income on demand. Heydo is building that identity rail — starting with Kerala.**

A gig marketplace is the **wedge**. The **moat** and the **endgame** is becoming the **trust infrastructure of the informal economy**: the verified-identity + reputation + payments layer that everyone — workers, households, businesses, even *other* platforms — plugs into.

---

## The singularity: how a gig app becomes infrastructure

Three compounding loops, each one harder to copy than the last:

### Loop 1 — The Identity Loop (Year 0–1)
Every worker passes **live VKYC**. Each verification is a permanent, non-replicable data asset: identity + skills + location + availability. A competitor starting today starts at zero trust. **This is the first wall.**

### Loop 2 — The Reputation Loop (Year 1–2)
Every completed gig adds to a worker's **Heydo Score** — ratings, on-time rate, gigs done, specialties, badges. Dual-side ratings mean the data is honest. The Score becomes a **real-world credential**: workers put it on résumés; contractors hire offline because of it. Once a worker's livelihood depends on their Score, **they never leave.** The platform becomes *life infrastructure, not an app.*

### Loop 3 — The Money Loop (Year 2+)
Earnings flow into a **Heydo wallet**. Add micro-savings, instant withdrawal, a co-branded **RuPay card**, micro-credit underwritten by verified income history. Heydo becomes the **neobank for the informal economy** — the path from gig take-rate to financial-services margin.

> Identity → Reputation → Money. Each loop feeds the next. By the time a competitor copies the gig features, we own the **trust graph** of Kerala's workforce.

## Why this can't be copied (the durable moats)

| Moat | Why it holds |
|---|---|
| **VKYC data network** | 2 years of verified identities + work history. Can't be backfilled. |
| **Heydo Score as credential** | Switching cost = your career. Workers won't abandon their reputation. |
| **Government partnership** | A Kudumbashree / Kerala-govt MoU is near-impossible for a Bangalore startup to replicate. ~3-year head start. |
| **Malayalam network effect** | Every Malayalam WhatsApp group recommending Heydo is organic supply/demand money can't buy. |

## The endgame (what "winning" looks like)

1. **Kerala:** the default way households hire and workers earn. The Heydo Score is on résumés.
2. **India:** the verified-worker identity rail. Other platforms *license* Heydo verification.
3. **Infrastructure:** Heydo Score is to informal labour what CIBIL is to credit — the trust number everyone checks.

## How to use this document

- **Sovereigns:** when prioritizing your roadmap, ask "does this deepen Loop 1, 2, or 3, or strengthen a moat?" If it does none, it's probably a distraction.
