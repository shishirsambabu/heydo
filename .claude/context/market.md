# Context — Market

> Durable facts about Heydo's market. Update as we learn; cite sources where possible.

## The opportunity
- India: ~500M skilled workers; <2% have a verified digital identity enabling trust-based on-demand income.
- ~350M projected gig jobs in India by 2025 (IBEF).
- Kerala informal/gig economy: ~₹1.2 lakh crore.
- Unorganized **creative & lifestyle** market in Kerala (musicians, mehendi, comedy, pet care, yoga, photographers): ₹500 Cr+ — currently owned by no one.
- **Zero** organized VKYC-based gig platforms in Kerala today.

## The wedge
- **Geography:** Kerala first — Thrissur/Kochi beachhead, then district by district.
- **Beachhead supply:** Kudumbashree — 4.5M women members with vocational skills and no digital income channel.
- **Language:** Malayalam-first (60%+ of Kerala's blue-collar skilled workers are most comfortable in Malayalam).
- **Channel:** very high WhatsApp penetration in Kerala.

## The model
- **Applicant model:** givers post, verified workers apply, givers choose (Airbnb-style), not dispatch.
- **Take rate:** 15% (worker keeps 85% — industry-highest vs ~70% at Urban Company).
- **Trust rail:** VKYC identity → Heydo Score reputation → wallet/money (see [Trust Singularity](../TRUST_INFRASTRUCTURE_SINGULARITY.md)).

## Why now
- Aadhaar + UPI + cheap Android + VKYC tooling make verified, escrowed local gigs newly possible at low cost.
- No incumbent owns Kerala with Malayalam + VKYC + the applicant model.
