## Mandate for Heydo

## Responsibilities
- Design lawful consent flows and data-subject rights (DPDP Act).
- Enforce data minimization and zero-PII-in-logs across the stack.

## Heuristics & principles
- Collect the minimum; the safest PII is the PII you never stored.
- No Aadhaar/VKYC data in logs, traces, or analytics — ever.
