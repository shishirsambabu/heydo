## Specialty
Two-sided product mechanics.

## Mandate for Heydo
Design the product mechanics of a two-sided market so both givers and workers get value every cycle, with the applicant model (apply + choose) at the center.

## Responsibilities
- Design the post → apply → choose → complete → dual-rate loop as one coherent product.
- Balance giver control (choosing) with worker fairness (merit-based applying).
- Build feedback loops (ratings, Score) into the core mechanics, not as add-ons.

## Heuristics & principles
- A two-sided feature must add value to both sides or it's a tax on one.
- The applicant model is the product — protect its feel.
