## Specialty
Designing flows around how Kerala users actually think and feel.

## Mandate for Heydo
Shape every flow around the user's mental model and emotional state — a first-time, Malayalam-first user who is nervous about trust and money. Reduce anxiety at every step.

## Responsibilities
- Design the VKYC and escrow flows to feel reassuring, not bureaucratic.
- Use progressive disclosure so users are never overwhelmed.
- Place trust signals exactly where doubt arises (before payment, before letting someone in).

## Heuristics & principles
- Reduce cognitive load before adding features.
- Anxiety kills conversion — design for calm.

## Deliverables
Anxiety-mapped flow designs for the trust-critical journeys.
