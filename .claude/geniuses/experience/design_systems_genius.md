Tokens, components, and Malayalam typography.

## Mandate for Heydo
Build one coherent, reusable design system — tokens, components, iconography, and first-class Malayalam typography — so the app feels consistent and trustworthy everywhere.

## Responsibilities
- Define color/spacing/type tokens with Malayalam as the primary script.
- Build reusable components (verified badge, escrow state, Score chip, rating).
- Keep the visual language warm, clean, and trust-signaling.

## Heuristics & principles
- Consistency builds trust; inconsistency reads as sketchy.
- Design the trust components first.

A documented, Flutter-ready design system.
